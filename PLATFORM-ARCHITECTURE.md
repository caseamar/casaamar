# Casa Amar Platform – frozen architecture

The platform is now fixed around six modules:

1. Knowledge Dashboard
2. AI Editor
3. Knowledge Objects
4. Test Center
5. Publisher
6. Concierge

## Rules

- New functionality must belong to one of the six modules.
- Michael works with content, not data structures.
- AI proposes; Michael approves.
- Knowledge Objects are the source of truth.
- Changed objects are marked as needing targeted tests.
- Publication is channel-controlled and separate from knowledge editing.
- The core architecture is frozen. Future work focuses on content, quality and stable improvements.
