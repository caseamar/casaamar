fix(ai): reduce unnecessary follow-up questions and hand off earlier

Summary
- Limited Casa Amar Concierge to one follow-up question per topic.
- Added a hard rule that follow-up questions are only allowed when the knowledge base can answer the next step.
- Prevented the concierge from repeating or rephrasing questions already asked.
- Added context handling for short replies such as "ja gerne".
- Removed unnecessary follow-up requirements from arrival, activities and family intents.
- Added earlier handoff to Michael when exact directions, address, access details or other unavailable information are required.
- Added a programmatic safeguard that suppresses a second follow-up question in the same topic.
- Preserved natural answers and internal use of secondary sources without exposing Rincón Rent.

Files changed
- index.html
- _worker.js
- concierge-config.json

Why
The concierge should feel helpful and decisive. It must answer with available information, avoid interviewing the guest unnecessarily, and move quickly to mail or Messenger when a human answer is more appropriate.

Test cases
- Hvordan kommer jeg til huset?
- Ja gerne.
- Kan du give præcis kørselsvejledning?
- Hvad kan vi lave i området?
- Vi kommer med børn.
