fix(ai): route dynamic and approval-based questions directly to Michael

Summary
- Made booking availability and price questions deterministic human handoffs.
- Added a separate pet_approval intent so dog and pet requests always require Michael's approval.
- Made current-information questions such as tomorrow's events and live opening hours immediate handoffs.
- Added a clear general fallback for requests outside Casa Amar's supported scope.
- Prevented these handoff responses from asking follow-up questions or showing irrelevant links.
- Kept ordinary house-rule questions available to the AI while isolating pet approval as a human decision.

Files changed
- index.html
- _worker.js
- concierge-intents.json

Why
The concierge should be excellent at what it knows and move quickly to Michael when information is dynamic, requires approval, or falls outside its scope. This update reduces friction and prevents the AI from pretending it can check live availability, approve pets, or provide real-time local information.

Test cases
- Er huset ledigt i oktober?
- Må vi tage en hund med?
- Hvad sker der i Fuengirola i morgen?
- Kan du hjælpe med noget helt andet?
